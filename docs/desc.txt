Initial release of Mountain Weather — an offline-ready weather app designed for adventurers in areas with poor connectivity.

• Current weather with temperature, wind, humidity, precipitation and pressure
• Hourly forecast (24h) and daily forecast (3 or 5 days)
• Search for any location or use GPS
• Save favorite and recent locations (up to 10 each)
• Offline mode — cached data available without internet
• Resilient sync
• Configurable background sync for favorite locations
